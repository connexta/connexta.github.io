Leadership

Leadership and Co-Founders

We founded Connexta to provide solutions that advance intelligence in the 21st century. Connexta develops secure, distributed intelligence processing capabilities. We break down third party proprietary restrictions from sensors enabling information sharing. Connexta specializes in the secure integration of data, applications, and services with a proven track record of supporting DoD, IC and international partners. The Connexta team is passionate about building software solutions in partnership with our customers; we are committed to technical excellence and quality.

Harold (Andy) Goodson

Chief Executive Officer

Mr. Andy Goodson leads Connexta with a focus on continued advancement of open source solutions to unlock and share complex information. With an emphasis on the global Intelligence Community globally, Mr. Goodson pursues a non-traditional model to defense contracting as a means to establish interoperability for U.S. national security agencies and our coalition partners. Prior to Connexta, Mr. Goodson was a principal at Lockheed Martin working with the Intelligence Community on sensor integration initiatives. Mr. Goodson also volunteers as a member on the board of directors for the Codice Foundation. The non-profit foundation is an industry-driven consortium comprised of government, academia, and industry, providing infrastructure, support, education, and export control advice to communities of government-focused engineers and organizations operating open source projects.

John Murphy

Chief Operations Officer

Mr. John Murphy has devoted his career to building and deploying innovative capabilities to the warfighter. As co-founder and COO for Connexta, Mr. Murphy is responsible for company staffing aspects including recruitment, career development, training, security and export compliance. Early in his career, he was a software developer building signals processing, satellite simulation, and imagery exploitation applications. Mr. Murphy then served in various development team leadership roles primarily in the C4ISR domain, for customers such as US Navy, US Air Force, and national agencies. Mr. Murphy has extensive experience working closely with customers, writing proposals, fielding systems, and providing technical direction to the development staff.

Michael Menousek

Chief Technology Officer

Mr. Michael Menousek guides the development and incorporation of open source enterprise and web technologies that contribute to intelligence mission success while maintaining an amazing user experience. Michael leads Connexta’s technical team with a passion for building cutting-edge software and sharing with others in an open, collaborative way. His technical career focus has been on designing extensible data integration frameworks and he is a specialist in the use and licensing of Free and Open Source Software (OSS). Michael has been recognized for developing innovative solutions for enterprise integration and secure discovery and retrieval of ISR across a variety of large organizations including the National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency, US Air Force, UK Ministry of Defence, Australian Defence Force, and US Coast Guard. Throughout the bulk of his career, his primary mission domain has been ISR with a particular focus on GEOINT domain. Michael serves as the Project Management Committee (PMC) Chair for the Codice Foundation’s Distributed Data Framework (DDF) project.
