Social Responsibility

Contributing back to our community is in our Connexta DNA

Vanessa Davis

Office Manager

“Corporate social responsibility is a priority at Connexta. It is embedded in our culture. Our employees are making a difference in the communities we sit in, and we are helping to expand impact by organizing volunteer opportunities, matching employee charitable donations and promoting programs like Dollars for Doers. I am proud to be a part of Connexta!
Prior to joining Connexta in January 2019, I worked for a decade in the Julie Ann Wrigley Global Institute of Sustainability at Arizona State University. Working horizontally across an array of disciplines gave me an appreciation for systems thinking and the power of collaborating to advance solutions. Now I am applying that experience in my role as Office Manager at Connexta to grow and evolve our office practices and employee engagement across the United States.”

Connexta Gives Back

Connexta employees are often looking to give back to their community, spurring the launch of two new programs in 2018 to assist employees in giving back and facilitating company-wide fundraising events.

Charitable Match

Connexta matches employee contributions to charitable organizations, up to $200 per year.

Dollars for Doers

Connexta matches volunteer hours with a charitable donation. Employees log volunteer hours and can earn up to $750 per year to benefit a charitable organization.
